CSS Sticky & Fixed Positions

Using position: sticky & fixed

I have a position Sticky!
📞

Scroll down to see the sticky and fixed elements in action.

The sticky element will stick to the top of the viewport when you reach its scroll position, while the fixed element will remain in place regardless of scrolling.

Note: The sticky element will only stick within its parent container, while the fixed element will stay fixed relative to the viewport.

You can also experiment with different values for the top, bottom, left, and right properties to see how they affect the positioning of the elements.

Remember to test the sticky and fixed elements in different browsers, as their behavior may vary slightly.
